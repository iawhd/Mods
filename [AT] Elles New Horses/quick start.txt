HOW TO USE

Before starting your game:
1. Put this folder in your stardew "Mods" folder. The folder path should be something like:
"C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Stardew Valley\Mods\"

In game:
1. Go to Robin's and buy "scissors" from the shop.
2. With the scissor tool selected, right click on the horse when it is on your farm.
3. Find the texture you want. You can navigate by clicking the arrow keys or typing keywords in the search bar in the top right.
4. Click the image you like to select a texture.
